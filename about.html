Since I wrote the first line of code, my smile is much more frequent.
Here is a brief summary of my life!

1996

In Cellino San Marco, a small town in southern Puglia, I was born and raised. Only a few kilometres from my country is the sea and the frequent sunny days help me to think positively. Despite all, I felt I wanted to escape from such a small place. The need to know new places, different cultures was growing within me. I wanted to broaden my vision of life, I wanted to grow and mature. So immediately after finishing high school, I decided to follow this thought.

2016

Backpacking and plane ticket to Barcelona, ​​a course for a notorious Bartender training school was waiting for me. The emotions were strong almost inexplicable, meeting people from all over the world allowed me to expand my horizons. So I started working in this sector, improving more and more interpersonal relationships with clients and colleagues.

2018/2020

After various personal and work experiences, I decided to start another experience in Dublin, driven by wanting to learn English spent a year in this wonderful country, I must say that the intent was successful. Once back in my home country I had the opportunity to work as a pizza chef. The commitment was constant, so much so that only after a year, I found myself independently managing a pizzeria in Milan. This experience has developed in me a strong sense of responsibility. After all, I still couldn't find my way.

2021/2022

Just during this year, I decided to look into the world of web development, I was intrigued to know something more about this field, even if I didn't have the slightest idea how it worked. I enrolled in the start2impact training school, its practical projects, corrected by professionals in the sector, gave me the opportunity to correct my mistakes. Even his guides on the online platform gave me a great help not to skip any topic. The passion in this sector is growing more and more, and I strongly believe that I have found my way.

" The future belongs to those who believe in the beauty of their dreams "
